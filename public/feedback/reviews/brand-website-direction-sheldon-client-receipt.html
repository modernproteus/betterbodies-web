BetterBodies TX Review Summary

Sheldon’s Brand Feedback Review

Thank you for the thoughtful feedback. This page reflects what I heard from the Brand + Website Direction survey and how I plan to translate it into the BetterBodies website and brand updates.

Quick Read

The broader BetterBodies direction is confirmed. CPR + BLS should still lead the site, while I’ll make sure the brand also communicates heart, community, diversity, positivity, crisis readiness, and practical safety training.

CPR + BLS lead the site Add de-escalation Diversity + unity Positive energy 25+ years Certifications + reviews

What I Heard

BetterBodies is personal.

This is a calling, not just a business. The site should make people feel that BetterBodies genuinely loves and cares about the people it serves.

CPR + BLS should lead.

The broader brand direction feels right, but CPR and BLS should stay clear, visible, and easy to act on.

The visuals should feel positive and diverse.

Show unity, real people, all races, and community energy. Avoid intimidating, mean-looking, or overly polished model-style imagery.

De-escalation belongs in Crisis Readiness.

De-escalation should be added into the Crisis Readiness language rather than treated as a separate service track for now.

Core message I captured from the feedback: BetterBodies should feel like it is built from the heart — personal, positive, community-rooted, and focused on helping people protect the people and places they care about.

Direction Confirmed

Brand direction
Answer: “Yes, this feels right.”
How I understand it: BetterBodies can be positioned as a broader readiness training brand, while keeping CPR + BLS as the clearest front-facing service priority.
Service tracks
Answer: “Yes, this structure feels right.”
How I understand it: Emergency Response, Crisis Readiness, Personal Safety, and Group Training still make sense as the working brand structure.
Homepage priority
Answer: CPR + BLS Certification.
How I understand it: The homepage should keep CPR and BLS highly visible, especially in the services, schedule, and future dedicated service page.
Trust signals
Answer: Certifications, positive comments/remarks, 25+ years, and community experience should help build trust.
How I understand it: The site should make proof and credibility easier to find.

Updates I’ll Make

Hero message

You suggested adding the idea of “protect your loved ones and your community.”

How I’ll use this: I’ll revise the hero direction to incorporate the “protect your loved ones and your community” idea so the message feels more personal, emotional, and true to the heart behind BetterBodies.

Hero directions I’ll explore:

Train your body. Prepare your mind. Protect your loved ones and your community.
Recommended working direction: Train your body. Prepare your mind. Protect what matters.
Be ready for the people who count on you.
Train with heart. Respond with confidence. Protect your community.
Confidence to care. Skills to protect.

Supporting copy

Working subhead direction
Founder-led CPR, BLS, first aid, de-escalation, crisis readiness, and personal safety training for individuals, teams, and communities across Central Texas.

Services

  • Keep CPR + BLS as the clearest service priority.
  • Add de-escalation into the Crisis Readiness language.
  • Plan the first dedicated service page as combined CPR + BLS Certification.

Visual direction

The current preview is intentionally light on final imagery while the brand foundation is being finalized. The next feedback forms, especially Photo + Content Inventory, Logo Review, and Voice + Messaging, will help add stronger visual presence using real BetterBodies photos, approved concepts, stock placeholders where needed, and custom branded graphics.

Visual concepts to prioritize

Sheldon teaching, coaching, smiling, and encouraging; diverse groups training together; real students and real body types; hands-on CPR/BLS practice; community settings; positive group energy.

Visuals to avoid

Intimidating solo “tough guy” shots, aggressive/tactical posing, overly perfect fitness-model imagery, scary/unapproachable visuals, or imagery that lacks racial/body-type diversity.

About / story

  • Bring forward that BetterBodies is a calling, not just a business.
  • Highlight 25+ years of community-based training.
  • Make the care, heart, and personal approach clearer.
  • Represent the teaching style as energetic, personal, honest, practical, and high-energy.

Confirmed Decisions

De-escalation will be listed as part of Crisis Readiness, not as a separate service track for now.
The first dedicated service page should be combined CPR + BLS Certification.
